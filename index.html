Mix Fruits Detox Water

Wash and chop a mix of fresh fruits like apple and grapes. Add them to a bottle of clean water and let it infuse for a few hours in the refrigerator. Shake lightly before drinking for a refreshing, naturally flavored detox water.

Alkaline Water

Add a few drops of fresh lemon juice and a pinch of baking soda to a bottle of clean, filtered water. Shake well until fully dissolved and let it sit for a minute. Drink fresh for a lightly alkaline, refreshing hydration boost.
